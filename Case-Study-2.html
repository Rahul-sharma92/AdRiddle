Building a pet care Brand on Ecommerce platforms

Powered by AdRiddle

Overview

A premium pet grooming brand approached AdRiddle to launch on Amazon and Flipkart with the aim of becoming a go-to name in pet care. Entering a competitive market with a niche product line, the brand needed a strong start to drive visibility, trust, and sales. AdRiddle stepped in by setting up and launching the brand on Amazon and Flipkart, optimizing listings, and implementing early-stage sales strategies that drove conversions from the very first week.

Initial Situation

  • The brand had zero presence across e-commerce platforms or digital channels.
  • Operating in a high-CPC category made early paid campaigns financially risky.
  • No brand awareness meant a major challenge in generating impressions and trust.
  • Unoptimized listings led to no organic visibility or traction at launch.
  • Immediate conversions were crucial to establish market fit and sustain momentum.

Challenges

  • Limited budget restricting large-scale marketing and advertising efforts.
  • No organic visibility due to lack of optimized listings and brand presence.
  • Overcoming high advertising costs due to elevated CPC rates in the category.
  • Building brand trust and awareness from scratch without prior customer base.
  • Generating early sales momentum to justify ongoing marketing investment.
